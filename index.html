FAQ


Do you use generative AI in your games?

No. We dont use any generative AI in the development of our games as we geniunly enjoy every part of game development and see no point in delogating develpment tasks to AI.

Availability

When will the game be available?

The game will be available as soon as possible. Unfortunately though its quite hard to tell when it will but we aim to be done by the summer of 2027.

Were can I play the game?

The game will be made available on the Steam platform upon release. Depending on the games success it may also be launched on additional platforms such as varius consoles.

How much will the game cost?

Were currently unsure of the exact price the game will have but we aim to keep the price relativly low to encourage a larger player base :D

Does the game require an Internet Connection?

Will the game have multiplayer?

Upon release the game will not have multiplayer support. However we would very much like to add multiplayer support in a future update.

How many upgrade items will the game have?

Were currently aiming for roughly 100 unique items.

Which languages will the game be available in?

Upon release the game will feature 8 languages, English (US), Swedish, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese (Brazil), Simplified Chinese, Japanese

Will the game have controller support?

Yes! The game will feature controller support, including (on pc) support for Sony Dualshock, Sony Dualsense, Xbox One and Series X/S Controllers, Nintendo Switch Joy-Cons.

Yes! By defualt all save data is saved to Steam Cloud.
