FAQ

Do you use generative AI in your games?

No. We dont use any generative AI in the development of our games as we geniunly enjoy every part of game development.

When will the game be available?

The game will be available as soon as possible. Unfortunately though its quite hard to tell when it will but we aim to be done by the summer of 2027.

Will the game have multiplayer?

Upon release the game will not have multiplayer support. However we would very much like to add multiplayer support in a future update.

Were can I play the game?

The game will be made available on the Steam platform upon release. Depending on the games success it may also be launched on additional platforms such as varius consoles.
